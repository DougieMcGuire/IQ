About BrainDeer

A daily brain-training app designed to keep your mind sharp — one game at a time.

🧠 What is BrainDeer?

BrainDeer is a free brain-training web app that helps you build mental fitness through short, engaging daily challenges. From word puzzles to math sprints and memory games, there's always something to sharpen your thinking.

We track your progress, adapt to your skill level, and reward consistency — just like a gym for your brain, but way more fun.

Wordle-style puzzles

Beat the clock with rapid-fire arithmetic challenges.
🎨
Color Sort
Sort and sequence colors to train visual logic.
⚡
Reaction Training
Test and improve your reaction speed over time.

📈 How it works

Sign up for free, complete a short calibration test, and BrainDeer will build a personalised training plan based on your strengths and weaknesses. Each session takes under 5 minutes — perfect for a daily habit.

Your IQ score adapts as you play, giving you a real-time picture of where you're improving and where to focus next.

🏆 Compete and grow

Climb the global leaderboard, maintain your daily streak, and earn XP with every session. BrainDeer is built around the same engagement loops that make the world's best learning apps addictive — because consistency is what actually makes you smarter.

🔒 Who we are

BrainDeer is an independent project built by a small team passionate about cognitive fitness and accessible education. We're not a big corporation — just people who believe a few minutes of focused mental exercise every day can genuinely change how you think.

Questions? We'd love to hear from you at contact@braindeer.org.
