About This App

Welcome to the Infinite Brain Teasers app! This app generates brain teasers automatically so you can challenge your mind endlessly.

How It Works

Each question is generated on the fly. You can track your streaks, test your logic skills, and try to beat your own high score!

Why We Made This

The goal is to provide a fun, and educational experience that keeps your brain sharp. Whether you have a few minutes or a few hours, you can keep challenging yourself.
